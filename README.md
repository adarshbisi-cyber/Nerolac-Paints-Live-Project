## 📋 Project Overview

This repository contains the research report and final presentation for an **Industry Live Project** conducted in collaboration with **Kansai Nerolac Paints**, one of India's largest paint manufacturers. The project was undertaken by **Group Future** between February and April 2026.

The study analyses the Indian paint and coatings industry across eight structured sections — from historical context and market sizing to competitive dynamics, future megatrends, and actionable strategic recommendations — with Kansai Nerolac as the primary client audience.

## 📊 Key Findings

- 🇮🇳 **Market Size:** USD 11 Billion (2023) → projected USD 19.5 Billion by 2031 at **9.28% CAGR** — nearly 2× the global rate
- 📉 **Per Capita Gap:** India at **4.2 kg** vs China 7.2 kg, USA 15.8 kg, Germany 21 kg — the core structural upside case
- 🔥 **Birla Opus Disruption:** ₹10,000 Cr investment, 1,332 Mn litre capacity, 6 plants — the largest competitive entry in 30 years
- 📈 **Organised Sector:** 63% today → projected **70%+ by FY30** driven by VOC/EPR regulatory enforcement
- ⚡ **EV Opportunity:** EV coatings generate **60–70% higher revenue per vehicle** vs ICE — an inimitable moat via Kansai Japan technology
- 🏆 **Strategic Prize:** Kansai Nerolac can reach **₹15,000 Cr revenue by FY30** (vs ₹7,540 Cr in FY24) if the 6-point agenda is executed

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations Summary

Six recommendations for Kansai Nerolac, prioritised by Impact × Feasibility:

| Code | Recommendation | Priority | Timeline |
|------|---------------|----------|----------|
| **R-KN-1** | Secure EV OEM Agreements — 5-yr supply deals with Tata EV, Mahindra Electric, Ola, Ather | 🔴 Do First | FY25–26 |
| **R-KN-2** | Decorative Premiumisation — Kansai Japan premium sub-brand, West/South Tier 1 | 🔴 Do First | FY25–26 |
| **R-KN-3** | Elite Dealer Programme — Lock in top 5,000 dealers vs Birla Opus | 🔴 Do First | FY25–26 |
| **R-KN-4** | Phygital 'Colour Impressions' — 18 stores + Impressions App + Painter Loyalty | 🔴 Do First | FY26–28 |
| **R-KN-5** | 'Nerolac Nippon Green' Sustainability Brand — All premium SKUs low-VOC by FY27 | 🟡 Plan Later | FY26–27 |
| **R-KN-6** | Geographic Focus — Defend West/South; partner-led East India entry | 🟢 Fill-In | FY27–28 |

**Financial outcome:** ₹7,540 Cr (FY24) → **₹15,000 Cr by FY30** | EBITDA: **13.5% → 17%+**

## 🏢 About the Client

**Kansai Nerolac Paints Ltd.** is one of India's top five paint manufacturers, a subsidiary of **Kansai Paint Co., Japan**. Known for its dominance in automotive OEM coatings (~40% market share) and a growing decorative presence, Kansai Nerolac holds approximately 8% of India's total paint market with revenue of INR 7,540 Cr in FY24.

| Metric | Value |
